THE REASON TO LIVE

MON, 05 AUG AT 06:30 PM

The punishment to face after I stop breathing, passing burdens onto people leaving them in pain seething, the gifted oath restricting hands from causing harm... if you try then I must die as if broken again I won't get that back, if stays there one more pulse in my body then kill me again physically for I cannot face that detest towards my being. I sometimes deem that worse than my grave's black...

Can't say lives will be better without me... I know a selfish step would ruin them more. Don't look for me for I am always hiding. Afraid of pain that comes with the cold steel blade cutting through the skin yet after every misery that's the first thing I start thinking.

What's the point of the failed attempts?

To only be left with a wasted promise, wasted time, damn wasted resources... A drama caused and left more regretful than before facing the bitter aversion in someone's eyes.

What's a reason to live? What's mine? If not just some strings of mere fears keeping me from becoming a limp flesh.
