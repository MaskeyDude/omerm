I remember looking out at mountains far looking like soft blue shadows turning golden through the breaks of dusk. I remember being there while the wind coming through opened windows caused a lot of noise. I remember the warm hands rubbing my cold stiff ones while her face found rest on my shoulder. I remember the car not moving slower. Though I recall how only the slowed time remembers my mind invariant stood the bluster yet with her, how silent remained my mind.
